Communication gets easier...

For everyone.

According to the Canadian Association for the Deaf, approximately 3.6% of the population are sign language users. For deaf Canadians who primarily communicate through American Sign Language (ASL), the primary language for the culturally deaf, day-to-day communication that hearing individuals often take for granted can be challenging.

To address this issue, we developed Voxio, a tool designed to streamline communication with hard-of-hearing and deaf individuals. Unlike the cumbersome nature of typing or writing responses, Voxio utilizes real-time ASL transcription and text-to-speech technology to facilitate a natural flow of conversation, mirroring the ease of physical interactions.
